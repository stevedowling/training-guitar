# Module 4 · Singing While Playing

> 📝 **Not written yet** - when you get here, ask Claude to *"write module 4 of the guitar course"*.

**Builds on:** [Module 1 · The Practice Reset](../module-01-the-practice-reset/) · Parallel track to modules 2-3 - needs daytime (noise-friendly) practice slots.

## What you'll learn

- Why singing-while-playing fails (the same reason piano hands fight) - and the automation ladder that fixes it
- Drive the guitar part to true autopilot: the talk-test, then the hum-test, then words
- Map lyrics onto strums for one ultra-simple song at a time - two chords first, earned complexity later
- The campfire strumming patterns that survive divided attention

## Planned lessons

1. The coordination problem - why it's automation, not talent
2. The ladder: play → talk → hum → sing, on a two-chord song
3. Full accompaniment - your first sung-and-played song, start to finish
