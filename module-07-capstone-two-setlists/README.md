# Module 7 · Capstone: Two Setlists

> 📝 **Not written yet** - when you get here, ask Claude to *"write module 7 of the guitar course"*. Best written after modules 5 and 6, tailored to the actual songs you finished along the way.

**Builds on:** everything - [The Campfire Set](../module-05-the-campfire-set/) and [The Family Band](../module-06-the-family-band/)

## What you'll do

Turn the repertoire into two real performances:

- **The living-room gig**: the duo set with your drummer, played for family, recorded
- **The campfire set**: sung and played for actual humans around actual fire (or a reasonable indoor facsimile)
- Performance skills both need: setlist flow, between-song patter, recovering gracefully, and the pre-gig run-through ritual
- The wrap-up: an honest re-audit against module 1's file - fragments then vs. finished songs now - and choosing what the *next* twelve songs will be

## Planned lessons

1. Performance prep - setlist order, transitions, and the run-through week
2. The gigs - both performances, recorded, debriefed kindly
3. The re-audit + what's next - the system is yours now
