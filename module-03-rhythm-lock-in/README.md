# Module 3 · Rhythm Lock-in

> 📝 **Not written yet** - when you get here, ask Claude to *"write module 3 of the guitar course"*.

**Builds on:** [Module 2 · First Full Songs](../module-02-first-full-songs/)

## What you'll learn

- Play *in time* rather than *near time* - locking to a metronome, drum loops, then a live drummer
- Count-ins, starts, and endings - the band skills that make two players sound like a band
- Follow a drummer's tempo (even when it wobbles - especially when it wobbles; yours is nine)
- Dynamics as a duo skill: getting louder and quieter *together*

## Planned lessons

1. The grid - metronome and drum-loop practice that isn't boring
2. Starts, stops, and count-ins - the glue of playing together
3. First duo sessions - following a beginner drummer, and songs as jam games
