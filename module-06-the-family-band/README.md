# Module 6 · The Family Band

> 📝 **Not written yet** - when you get here, ask Claude to *"write module 6 of the guitar course"*.

**Builds on:** [Module 3 · Rhythm Lock-in](../module-03-rhythm-lock-in/)

## What you'll learn

- Arrange songs for a guitar + drums duo: covering the missing bass (power chords, palm-muted low strings), leaving space for the drums
- Pick and adapt songs *for the drummer you have* - her simplest steady beat should be the real drum part
- Run rehearsals a 9-year-old asks to repeat: short, song-first, games not drills, and she picks some of the setlist
- Record the duo simply, so progress is audible to both of you (and to grandparents)

## The motivational thesis

Getting a kid to practice an instrument is hard; getting her to *play in a band* is easy - and the band needs a guitarist who finishes songs. Modules 2-3 made you that guitarist. This module spends it.

## Planned lessons

1. Duo arranging - filling the space without a bassist
2. The rehearsal game - structures that beat "go practice"
3. The duo setlist - three songs tight enough to play for an audience of grandparents
